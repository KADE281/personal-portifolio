About Me

Student  |  Developer  |  Problem Solver

Discover my journey, passions, and what drives me as a computer science enthusiast.

I am a Computer Science student at the University of Rwanda with a strong passion for backend development, artificial intelligence, and mobile applications.

I enjoy turning complex problems into clean, efficient solutions — whether that's building REST APIs, training ML models, or crafting mobile experiences with Flutter.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring new technologies, contributing to open source, or sharpening my problem-solving skills on competitive platforms.

My Journey

I started my programming journey in high school with Java and C++, quickly realizing my passion for solving real-world problems through code. Over the years, I've expanded my skill set to cover full-stack web development, data structures, algorithms, and emerging technologies like AI and machine learning.

My academic path at University of Rwanda has been instrumental in deepening my understanding of computer science principles. I've worked on various projects ranging from desktop GUI applications using Java Swing to responsive web interfaces with modern frontend frameworks.
